There is a lot to love about this classic whiskey and bitters beverage. The offical drink of New Orleans, the Sazerac is every bit as aromatic and flavorful as most anything from down on the bayou.

Preperation Time

  • Total: Approximately 5 minutes
  • Preperation: 5 minutes

Nutritional Facts

  • Calories:152
  • Carbs:4g
  • Protein:0g

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • 3 dashes of Peychaud's Bitters
  • 2 dashes of Angostrua Bitters
  • 1 sugar cube
  • 1 cup of ice cubes
  • 2 fl oz of rye whiskey
  • 1 lemon peel

Steps

  1. Fill a rocks glass with 1 cup ice and set aside to chill for about 5 minutes.Discard ice once glass is cold.
  2. Combine Peychaud's Bitters, Angostrua Bitters and sugar cube in a cocktail shacker; mash well with a cocktail muddler. add 1 cup of ice and whiskey. Stir until chilled and strain into the chilled glass.
  3. Twist a lemon peel over the surface of the cocktail to extract the essence; discard lemon peel.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
