Buttermilk Fried Chicken

This Buttermilk fried chicken is incredibly tender and tangy.After the buttermilk soak, cover the chicken pieces in well seasoned flower and proceed to fry them in hot oil until crisp and golden

Preperation Time

  • Total: Approximately 7 hours
  • Preperation: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 25 minutes

Nutritional Facts

  • Calories:1262
  • Carbs:56g
  • Fat:85g
  • Protein:66g

Ingredients

For the Chicken

  • Whole chicken(cut into eight pieces)
  • Black pepper-(1 tsp)
  • White pepper-(1/2 tsp)
  • Salt-(1 tsp)
  • Paprika-(1 tsp)
  • Dried rosemary-(1/4 tsp)
  • Ground thyme-(1/4 tsp)
  • Dried oregano-(1/4 tsp)
  • Dried sage-(1/4 tsp)
  • Cayenne Pepper-(1/4 tsp)
  • Penut oil-(2 1/2 quarts)
  • Buttermilk

For the seasoned flour

  • All purpose flour-(2 cups)
  • Salt-(1 tsp)
  • Paprika-(1/2 tsp)
  • Cayenne pepper-(1/2 tsp)
  • Garlic powder-(1/2 tsp)
  • White pepper-(1/2 tsp)
  • Onion powder-(1/2 tsp)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Toss the chicken in the seasoning, in a large bowl or dish.
  3. Pour buttermilk in to the dish with the seasoned chicken and stir until the chicken is evenly coated.
  4. Combine the flour,salt,paprika,cayenne,garlic powder,white pepper, and onion powder in a large shallow dish.
  5. Remove the chicken from the buttermilk and cover eatch piece in the seasoned flour, and sake off any excess. Then transfer to a cooking pan.
  6. Proceed to heat the peanut oil to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C).
  7. Transfer the fried chicken to a cooling rack set over a paper towel lined baking sheet. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes before serving.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
